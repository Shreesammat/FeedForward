Feed Forwards



Our goal is to leverage a way to help reduce the wastage of food in Restaurants by sharing it with people who can't afford it. Become a Volunteer to help distribute the foods to the nearby communities who are in need of it.

How to be a Volunteer

Any interested people can extend their hand to help us in Volunteering foods to the poor communities. To become Volunteer sign up to our website and fill up the Volunteer form.
